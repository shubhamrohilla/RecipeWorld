Magic cake

Magic cake is one of the tastiest cakes in the world ever known. Few have tried it and lived to tell about it,and these are their stories. You can also review our Legal Page for more information.

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 Pound of Sugar
  • 3 Sticks of Butter

Directions

  1. Mix eggs,Sugar and butter in a large Bowl
  2. Spreaf into a non stick dish
  3. Bake at 350 degree for 1 hour
  4. Let sit at room temprature for 20 minutes
  5. Eat and enjoy !
